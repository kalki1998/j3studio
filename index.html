    SERVICES

    What we offer


    Preparation of Presentation Drawing

    Graphical representation to visually communicate the design concept.

    Approval Drawings

    Detailed drawings submitted for approval by relevant authorities or clients.

    Working Drawings

    Detailed plans used by contractors for construction, specifying materials, dimensions, and procedures.



    Landscape Drawings

    Plans illustrating the design of outdoor spaces, including plants, paths, and features.

    Completion Drawings

    Final as-built drawings reflecting the completed project, including any modifications made during construction.

    Structural Drawings

    Detailed drawings of the structural elements, like beams and columns, ensuring the building's stability.

    Electrical Drawings

    Schematics and layouts for electrical systems, including wiring, lighting, and power distribution.

    Mechanical Drawings

    Diagrams showing mechanical systems such as HVAC, refrigeration, and ventilation.

    Plumbing Drawings

    Layouts depicting the plumbing system, including water supply, drainage, and fixtures.

    Fire Fighting Drawings

    Plans detailing the fire protection systems, including sprinklers, hydrants, and alarms.

    Interiors

    Drawings illustrating the design and layout of interior spaces, including furniture and finishes.

    Coordination of all Services

    Integrated drawings ensuring all building systems and services work together without conflicts.
